Selecting TF‑tuned neurons
Keep only neurons whose firing actually changes with temporal frequency (TF), then use those for area decoding (PM vs AL).
STEP 1
Raw tuning response
8 directions × 5 TFs = 40 conditions (blank‑subtracted mean).
STEP 2
Collapse to a TF curve
Average over the 8 directions → 5 numbers.
STEP 3
TF modulation depth
depth = Rmax − Rmin Rmax = 0.50 − 0.05 0.50
depth = 0.45 / 0.50 = 0.90
Weakest TF response is 90% below the strongest → strongly tuned.
Keep if depth ≥ threshold.
STEP 4
How the threshold is chosen
Sweep thresholds 0.00 → 0.75. At each: keep neurons above it → classify PM vs AL (logistic regression, session‑grouped CV) → record balanced accuracy. Pick the highest.
